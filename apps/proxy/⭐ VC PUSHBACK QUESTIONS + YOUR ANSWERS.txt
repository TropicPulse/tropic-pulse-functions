3. “Networks get slower with more users. How does yours get faster?”

- more shared state
- more shared work
- fewer redundant operations
- fewer repeated calls
So instead of 100 people doing 100× the work,
100 people do 1.2× the work.
That’s why PulseNet gets more stable as more users join.”
