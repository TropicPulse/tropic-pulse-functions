Questions, Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions about Tropic Pulse. Want to know more? Reach out anytime.

Tropic Pulse connects customers, vendors, and deliverers in one app. Customers browse and order, vendors manage their menus and offerings, and deliverers fulfill orders across San Pedro and beyond.
For customers, browsing and exploring is free. Vendors and deliverers participate under a simple, transparent fee structure designed to support local businesses and fair earnings.
Use the contact form below or email us directly with your business details. We’ll walk you through onboarding, menu setup, and payouts so you can start receiving orders quickly.
Payouts are handled via Stripe Connect, giving vendors and deliverers a secure, reliable way to receive funds. Specific payout schedules and options are discussed during onboarding.
Tropic Pulse is launching in San Pedro, Belize, with plans to expand across the country and into the wider Caribbean as we grow.
