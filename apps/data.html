Data Deletion

At Tropic Pulse, we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your personal information associated with the Tropic Pulse app and services.

What data can be deleted

We can delete:

Account information

Your name, email, phone number, profile details, and login identifiers.

App usage data

Favorites, saved places, reviews, ratings, and preferences tied to your account.

Communication history

Support messages and non-essential communication logs, where not required for legal or security reasons.

Some data may be retained where we are legally required or have a legitimate business need (for example, fraud prevention, accounting, or dispute resolution). In those cases, we minimize what is kept and how long it is stored.

How to request data deletion

From within the app (preferred)

  • Open the Tropic Pulse app
  • Go to Profile / Settings
  • Tap Privacy & Data
  • Select Delete My Account & Data
  • Follow the on-screen confirmation steps

By email

Send an email with the subject line “Data Deletion Request” to:
Sales@TropicPulse.bz

Include:

  • Full name
  • Email address used in the app
  • Phone number (if linked to your account)
  • A short statement:
    “I am requesting deletion of my Tropic Pulse account and all associated personal data.”

We may ask you to verify your identity to protect your account from unauthorized deletion.

What happens after you request deletion

Acknowledgment

We’ll confirm we received your request.

Processing time

We aim to complete deletion within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law or for security review.

Effect of deletion

  • You will lose access to your Tropic Pulse account.
  • Your profile, favorites, and other personal content will be removed or anonymized.
  • Some aggregated or de-identified data (for analytics and service improvement) may be retained, but it will no longer be linked to you.

Business and partner data

If you manage a business listing on Tropic Pulse:

  • Personal owner/admin data can be deleted as above.
  • Business listing data (name, location, public details) may remain visible if it is considered public business information, but we can:
    • Remove your personal contact details, and
    • Transfer or reassign listing ownership where appropriate.

If you need deletion or transfer related to a business listing, mention this clearly in your request.

Questions about your privacy

If you have questions before requesting deletion, or want to understand what data we hold about you, contact us at:

Email: Sales@TropicPulse.bz
Subject line: “Privacy Question – Tropic Pulse”
