1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Ford Family Distribution – Belize (operating as Tropic Pulse) (“Tropic Pulse”, “we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Platform, mobile app, and related services (“Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Our Terms of Use also apply to your use of the Platform and contain additional rules and responsibilities.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact details
  • Email address and phone number
  • Delivery address and location information
  • Order history and transaction details
  • Device information, IP address, and usage data
  • Preferences, feedback, and support messages
  • Cookies and tracking data

3. User Responsibilities (Account & Accuracy)

Users are responsible for ensuring that all information provided to Tropic Pulse is accurate and up to date. Users are also responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials.

4. Public Content (Reviews, Comments, and Posts)

Any content you submit publicly on the Platform—such as reviews, comments, or ratings—is not considered private information. Such content may be visible to other users and may be retained even after account deletion.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process orders and coordinate deliveries
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Verify accounts and ensure security
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Send promotional messages (only with separate opt‑in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, enhance performance, and analyze usage across the Platform.

Our Cookie Policy provides additional details about the types of cookies we use and how they operate.

  • Session Cookies – operate the Service
  • Preference Cookies – remember settings
  • Security Cookies – protect your account
  • Advertising Cookies – show relevant ads

7. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract
  • Your consent
  • Legitimate interests
  • Legal compliance

8. Retention of Data

We retain Personal Data only as long as necessary for operational, legal, and security purposes.

9. Transfer of Data

Your data may be transferred to servers outside Belize. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

10. Disclosure of Data

Business Transactions

Your data may be transferred if Tropic Pulse is involved in a merger or acquisition.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations or protect user safety.

11. Communications, SMS Messaging & Email Notifications

By providing your phone number and email address, you consent to receive communications from Tropic Pulse. These communications may include:

  • Order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Driver arrival notifications
  • Account verification and security alerts
  • Payment confirmations and receipts
  • Service announcements

11.1 SMS Text Messaging (Transactional)

We use Twilio to send SMS messages for essential service functions. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe from non‑essential messages.
Reply HELP for help.

Essential messages (delivery updates, security alerts) cannot be opted out of without affecting your ability to use the Service.

11.2 Marketing SMS Messaging (Separate Opt‑In)

Marketing or promotional SMS messages require a separate, explicit opt‑in. This consent must be provided through an unchecked checkbox or similar one‑click mechanism.

You may opt out of marketing messages at any time by replying STOP.

11.3 Required Mobile Data Privacy Statement

No mobile information (phone numbers, SMS opt‑in data, or message metadata) will be shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Information sharing with subcontractors strictly for the purpose of providing the Service (e.g., Twilio for SMS delivery) is permitted.

11.4 Email Notifications

Transactional emails cannot be unsubscribed from. Marketing emails may be opted out of using the unsubscribe link.

12. Your Data Protection Rights

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your data
  • Rectify inaccurate information
  • Object to processing
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent
