1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Tropic Pulse (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, improve, and personalize your experience on our website and mobile platform.

By continuing to browse or use our site, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy. For more information about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly, remember your preferences, and improve your overall experience.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Keep you logged in between pages
  • Support analytics and performance tracking
  • Improve user experience and site functionality
  • Support advertising and personalization

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site. The information collected is aggregated, anonymous, and cannot identify any individual user.

4. Disabling Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. However, doing so may affect the functionality of our website and may prevent certain features from working properly, including login, checkout, and preference settings.

5. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function. They enable core features such as security, page navigation, and access to protected areas.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences to enhance your experience.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. The data collected is anonymous and helps us improve performance and content.

Account & Login Cookies

If you create an account or log in, we use cookies to remember your session and keep you logged in between pages.

Form Cookies

When you submit information through forms (such as contact or support forms), cookies may remember your details for future correspondence.

6. Third‑Party Cookies

We may use cookies provided by trusted third parties, including:

  • Google Analytics – to analyze site usage and improve performance
  • Google AdSense / DoubleClick – to deliver relevant ads and limit ad repetition
  • Social Media Platforms – such as Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter

These cookies may track your browsing behavior across websites and help us show ads that match your interests.

7. Social Media Cookies

These cookies are used when you share content using social media buttons, link your account, or interact with our content on platforms such as Facebook, LinkedIn, or Twitter. They may track your browsing habits and help deliver personalized ads.
