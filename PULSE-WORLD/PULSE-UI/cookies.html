Tropic Pulse & Pulse‑World – Cookie Policy

Minimal Cookies. Maximum Privacy.

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Tropic Pulse and Pulse‑World (“we”, “our”, “us”) use cookies and similar technologies to operate our websites and apps.

We are a privacy‑minimal platform. We only use the cookies necessary to keep your session secure, remember basic preferences, and ensure the site works correctly.

For full details on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember who you are, keep you logged in, and function properly.

Cookies are not programs, cannot run code, and cannot access files on your device.

3. How we use cookies

ESSENTIAL COOKIES

We use essential cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in securely
  • Protect your account from fraud
  • Enable core features of the site
  • Remember basic preferences (language, theme)

OPTIONAL ANALYTICS (IF ENABLED)

If analytics are enabled, we may use privacy‑respecting analytics to understand how users interact with our site. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

WHAT WE DO NOT USE

  • No advertising cookies
  • No cross‑site tracking cookies
  • No third‑party ad networks
  • No fingerprinting or behavioral profiling

4. Types of cookies we use

4.1 Essential Cookies

Required for the website and app to function.

  • Authentication cookies
  • Security tokens
  • Session identifiers

4.2 Preference Cookies

Used to remember simple settings like language or UI preferences.

4.3 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Used only if analytics are enabled. These help us understand usage patterns in an anonymous, aggregated way.

4.4 No Advertising Cookies

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting cookies, or cross‑site tracking cookies.

5. Managing cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, essential cookies are required for login, security, and core functionality.

If you disable essential cookies, the site may not function correctly.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page.
