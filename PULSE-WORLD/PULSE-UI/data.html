Tropic Pulse & Pulse‑World – Data Deletion Request

Your Data. Your Control.

1. Overview

At Tropic Pulse and Pulse‑World, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. You can request deletion of your personal data at any time. This page explains what can be deleted, what must be retained, and how to submit a request.

2. What data can be deleted

2.1 Account information

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Profile image and display name
  • Login identifiers and authentication tokens

2.2 Pulse‑World identity & presence

  • Your Pulse‑Identity profile
  • Presence signals (online status, last active)
  • Badges, systemAge, and social metadata

2.3 Tropic‑Pulse usage data

  • Favorites, saved places, delivery addresses
  • Order history (where legally allowed)
  • Reviews, ratings, and public posts

2.4 Pulse‑Events

  • Event participation history
  • Event hosting data (where allowed)

2.5 Pulse‑Earn (optional workloads)

  • Workload participation logs
  • Performance metrics
  • Non‑financial Earn metadata

2.6 Communications

  • Support messages
  • Non‑essential communication logs

Some data must be retained where required by law (e.g., financial records, fraud prevention, tax compliance).

3. What cannot be deleted immediately

3.1 Financial & payout records

If you used Tropic‑Pulse for purchases or Pulse‑Earn for payouts, we must retain certain financial records for legal and tax compliance.

3.2 Fraud prevention & security logs

We may retain limited logs to protect the platform and prevent abuse.

3.3 Public business listings

If you manage a business listing:

  • We can delete your personal owner/admin data
  • But the public business listing may remain visible
  • We can transfer ownership to another authorized person

4. How to request deletion

4.1 From within the app (recommended)

  • Open the Tropic Pulse app
  • Go to Profile → Settings
  • Select Privacy & Data
  • Tap Delete My Account & Data
  • Follow the confirmation steps

4.2 By email

Send an email with the subject line “Data Deletion Request” to:
Sales@TropicPulse.bz

Include:

  • Your full name
  • Email used in the app
  • Phone number (if linked)
  • A statement: “I am requesting deletion of my Tropic Pulse account and all associated personal data.”

We may ask you to verify your identity to protect your account.

5. What happens after you request deletion

5.1 Acknowledgment

You will receive confirmation that your request was received.

5.2 Processing time

We aim to complete deletion within 30 days, unless additional time is required for legal or security review.

5.3 Effects of deletion

  • Your account will be permanently closed
  • Your Pulse‑Identity will be removed or anonymized
  • Your Tropic‑Pulse data (favorites, addresses, preferences) will be removed
  • Your Pulse‑Earn participation data will be deleted (except legally required financial records)
  • Some aggregated or anonymized data may be retained for analytics

6. Questions or concerns

If you have questions before requesting deletion, or want to understand what data we hold, contact us:

Email: Sales@TropicPulse.bz
Subject line: “Privacy Question – Tropic Pulse”
