Tropic Pulse & Pulse‑World – Privacy Policy

Private by Design

Effective Date

February 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Ford Family Distribution – Belize (operating as Tropic Pulse) (“Tropic Pulse”, “we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use:

TROPIC‑PULSE Belize delivery, events, and vendor marketplace.

PULSE‑WORLD Global social identity, presence, badges, systemAge, and cross‑app profile layer.

We are privacy‑minimal by design. We only collect what we need to operate the services and never sell your data. By using the Platform, you agree to this Policy and our Terms of Use.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account & identity information

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile image or avatar (optional)

2.2 Service & usage information

  • Delivery addresses (Tropic‑Pulse)
  • Order and transaction history
  • Event participation details
  • Presence and activity signals (online status, last active)
  • Device type, basic technical logs, and IP address for security

2.3 Location & Bluetooth

  • Location – Required for Tropic‑Pulse delivery and events; optional for Pulse‑World relevance.
  • Bluetooth – Optional; used only for Pulse‑World proximity presence when you enable it.

We do not collect background location, contact lists, microphone audio, or camera access except when you explicitly upload a photo.

2.4 Communications

  • SMS verification codes and delivery updates
  • Transactional emails (receipts, confirmations, security alerts)
  • Support messages and feedback

2.5 Earn & workloads (Pulse‑Earn)

  • Workload participation records (e.g., completed compute tasks)
  • Payout history and related identifiers required by payment partners

3. How we use your information

3.1 Core purposes

  • Provide, operate, and maintain Tropic‑Pulse and Pulse‑World
  • Process orders, deliveries, events, and payouts
  • Verify accounts, send PINs, and protect against fraud
  • Maintain identity, presence, and profile across the ecosystem
  • Provide support and respond to your requests

3.2 Improvements

  • Improve reliability, performance, and user experience
  • Monitor system health and prevent abuse

3.3 Marketing (only with consent)

  • Send marketing SMS or email only if you separately opt in
  • You can opt out at any time

We do not build advertising profiles, do not sell your data, and do not use your presence or location for targeted advertising.

4. Device permissions & what we do not collect

4.1 Permissions we use

  • Location – For deliveries, nearby events, and optional relevance in Pulse‑World.
  • Bluetooth – Optional; for proximity presence when enabled.

4.2 Permissions we do not use

  • No access to your contacts
  • No background location tracking
  • No microphone recording
  • No camera access except when you choose to upload a photo
  • No access to SMS inbox, call logs, or device files
  • No health, biometric, or sensitive category data

5. Cookies & minimal analytics

We use only essential and minimal functional cookies to keep you signed in, secure your session, and remember basic preferences. We do not run invasive ad‑tracking or third‑party advertising cookies.

  • Session cookies – keep you logged in and secure
  • Preference cookies – remember language or basic settings
  • Security tokens – protect against fraud and abuse

If we add analytics in the future, it will be privacy‑respecting and documented here.

6. Legal basis for processing (GDPR‑style)

  • Contract – To provide deliveries, events, identity, presence, and Earn workloads.
  • Consent – For marketing messages, optional Bluetooth, and optional location relevance.
  • Legitimate interests – To secure the platform, prevent fraud, and improve reliability.
  • Legal obligations – To comply with tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements.

7. Data retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to operate the services, comply with law, resolve disputes, and maintain security. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

  • Account and profile data – kept while your account is active.
  • Order and payout records – kept as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • Logs and security data – kept for a limited time for fraud prevention and diagnostics.

8. Data transfers

Our infrastructure may be hosted outside Belize, including in other countries. Where required, we use reasonable safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms) to protect your data. By using the Service, you consent to these transfers.

9. Disclosure of data

9.1 Business operations

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, SMS, payments) who process data on our behalf under strict contracts.
  • Delivery partners and vendors who need limited information to fulfill your orders or events.

9.2 Business transactions

If Tropic Pulse is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Policy.

9.3 Legal requirements

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or security of users, vendors, or the platform.

10. Communications, SMS & email

10.1 Transactional communications

By providing your phone number and email, you consent to receive essential communications, including:

  • Account verification and security alerts
  • Order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Driver arrival notifications
  • Event confirmations and reminders
  • Payment confirmations and receipts

10.2 SMS messaging (Twilio)

We use Twilio to send SMS messages for essential service functions. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe from non‑essential messages.
Reply HELP for help.

Essential messages (such as security alerts and critical delivery updates) may be required to use the Service.

10.3 Marketing SMS (separate opt‑in)

Marketing or promotional SMS messages require a separate, explicit opt‑in via an unchecked checkbox or similar one‑click mechanism. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

10.4 Required mobile data privacy statement

No mobile information (phone numbers, SMS opt‑in data, or message metadata) will be shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Information sharing with subcontractors strictly for the purpose of providing the Service (for example, Twilio for SMS delivery or hosting providers) is permitted.

10.5 Email notifications

Transactional emails (such as receipts, security alerts, and order confirmations) are part of the Service and cannot be fully unsubscribed from. Marketing emails, if any, will always include an unsubscribe link.

11. Pulse‑World, identity & presence

PULSE‑IDENTITY & PRESENCE

11.1 Unified identity

  • Your Pulse‑Identity may be used across Tropic‑Pulse, Pulse‑World, and related services.
  • Core fields include email, phone number, display name, and profile image.
  • This keeps your account consistent across the ecosystem.

11.2 Presence signals

  • Presence may show when you are online, recently active, or participating in an event.
  • Optional proximity presence may use Bluetooth when you enable it.
  • Presence is not used for advertising and is not sold to third parties.

11.3 Social content

  • Public posts, reviews, or visible profile elements may be seen by other users.
  • We may retain some public content even if you delete your account, where necessary for safety or legal reasons.

12. Pulse‑Earn & workloads

PULSE‑EARN

12.1 Nature of processing

  • Pulse‑Earn allows eligible users to participate in optional digital workloads (e.g., compute tasks, AI processing, or similar lawful activities).
  • We record participation, performance, and payout history to operate the service and comply with financial rules.

12.2 No employment relationship

Pulse‑Earn does not create an employer‑employee relationship. We do not process data as an employer; we process it to operate a contribution‑based platform and to comply with payment and tax obligations.

12.3 Data sharing for payouts

  • We may share limited information with payment partners (such as Stripe) to verify identity and process payouts.
  • We do not sell or broker your Earn data.

13. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights similar to GDPR/CCPA, including:

  • Access – request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction – ask us to fix inaccurate information.
  • Deletion – request deletion of your account and certain data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Restriction – request limited processing in certain cases.
  • Objection – object to certain types of processing.
  • Portability – request a copy of your data in a portable format where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent – for any processing based on consent (e.g., marketing).

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

14. Children’s privacy

Our services are intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

15. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Effective Date” at the top. If changes are significant, we may provide additional notice within the app or by email. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect means you accept the updated Policy.
